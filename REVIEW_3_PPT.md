## Slide 4: Project Overview
- LILNEST is designed as a single digital space for maternal wellness support.
- It combines medical guidance, wellness tools, and emotional support.
- The platform focuses on usability, accessibility, and pregnancy-stage-specific assistance.

## Slide 5: Problem Statement
- Pregnant users often need quick access to reliable guidance and daily wellness support.
- Existing solutions are fragmented across multiple apps and websites.
- There is a need for one integrated platform for tracking, learning, and support.

## Slide 6: Objectives
- Provide AI-based maternal assistance.
- Support community interaction and peer sharing.
- Offer pregnancy growth visualization and milestone tracking.
- Enable secure login and protected user data.

## Slide 7: Key Features
- AI Assistant for instant pregnancy-related guidance.
- Pregnancy Growth Visualizer for stage-wise fetal development.
- Time Capsule for storing memories and milestones.
- Community module for support and discussion.
- Marketplace for health-related service access.
